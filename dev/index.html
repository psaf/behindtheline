Art in Branding

Whether you want to create an area within your project of company ephemera, historical documents, donor wall, custom sculpture, room and way finding signage, didactics for your art collection, glass or metal wall installations or emphasize your company image, culture and values using your logo in creative ways, we source and coordinate with our network of trusted vendors, manufactures and artisans to create an overall branding concept and make it a reality.

Art Consultation

Our art consultants will meet with you to discuss and determine your overall concept. All of the following will be addressed and discussed: Colors, themes, environment, framing, timeline and deadlines. We can then present you with an art package based on your budget that contains area specific ideas and concepts as options for your primary and secondary locations. At your request this can also include framing options and concepts, branding and signage. Once you have approved the ideas and concepts in the art package we will coordinate all the details such as size, medium or materials, colors to be used, the timeline for creation, and deadlines for delivery by the artist or vendor.

Training Services

Community YouthMapping™

Community YouthMapping (CYM) is themapping of resources, priority issues, partners and opportunities in the community. Through this process youth learn how to collect, analyze, share and present data toward impacting outcomes. CYM has also facilitated the identification of opportunities for employment, education, training, internships, healthy food choices, and many others. The CYM training begins with the personal and professional benefits for youth and moves through the day to day skills and responsibilities using a variety of small and large group activities. Ultimately the youth gain skills in public speaking, data collection, data presentation and develop a strong grasp on their own roles and responsibilities in making CYM a success.

Youth Engagement

Youth Engagement is the intentional provision of opportunities for youth to be engaged in community/youth development. Successful youth engagement can develop leadership skills in youth, educate youth on priority issues, increase the courage and desire to "get involved", and solidify a strong lifelong connection between youth and the community. Youth Engagement training identifies the most common issues for youth in the community and explores barriers and facilitators for youth engagement. Through training youth develop a practical plan to engage themselves and others, while empowering them to making positive change in their community.

Youth Leadership

Youth Leadership focuses on the development of necessary skills for youth to successfully transition to adulthood. These skills includeself motivation, desire for self improvement, recognition of individual and community needs and resources. Youth Leadership training takes youth on a journey that identifies personal and community needs and begins to identify practical plans to address them. Through training youth gain confidence in their own skills, learn how to work in teams, and identify areas for self improvement.

Bias and Stereotype Awareness

With a constant media and information stream, youth are exposed to thousands of messages and ideas every time they log onto a computer or pick up their smart phone. How do they sort out the negative and harmful from the accurate and fair? Now more than ever youth need to build skills around media literacy and the science of bias and this session engages youth in a conversation about stereotypes and bias and their impacts.
